    About Us

    OData Corp is the favourite shop for people who love the outdoors, camping, climbing and winter/alpine sports. We have two retail outlets in Jogjakarta, Indonesia, as well as an online store which takes orders from all over the world. We stock high performance gear from the world's best brands. From waterproof jackets to walking boots, climbing equipment to trail running shoes we've everything you need to enjoy the outdoors.

    Our customers value the honest, friendly, helpful expert advice they get from our staff who actually use the gear constantly (yes, we are outdoor fanatics ourselves!) Our approach to customer services has won us multiple awards from the Outdoor Industry and Mystery Shopper Awards from the Retail Industry. Whatever your interest in the outdoors, we can get you the right kit at the best price!

    An independent store run by passionate people

    We are a technical independent outdoor store run by passionate people who live for the great outdoors! We pride ourselves on our first hand knowledge of products, our quality of customer service and our love of all things outdoor. Our expert staff are here to make sure you get the correct kit for the activities you enjoy. With 2 shops in Jogjakarta city centre we have the biggest and best range of mountain sports clothing, equipment and accessories and our prices are keen and competitive.
